You want to enter the castle but you need to collect 3 crowns first before you are allowed in. So you search for the crown while there is also a strange alien trying to kill you. You find out that the crowns are claimable once you complete a parkour. When you got 3 crowns and go in the castle you will be rich and forever happy.

About Project

Game Engine: Unity

Code language: C#

Platforms: Pc/Steamdeck/mobile/Quest 2&3

Time: 4 weeks

People: 1 dev

Description

I had to make a platformer game and i decide to just make a little open world and just put the parkours everywhere. Then i thought why not make a goal you need to reach. Then there was the castle and the crowns and after that you just win. We also needed to make the game on 3 different platforms. I made this game on the steamdeck wich is verily easy when you know the steps. I also made it on mobile with touch buttons just to experience how that works and at last vr wich was a bit different from normal vr games cause i put the camera in the surrounding and not on the players eyes. it was a lot of fun and i really enjoyed this one cause we did get a lot of freedom in this project.
