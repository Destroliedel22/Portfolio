Clown office

This is a VR experience about jasper the clown that needs to quit his job as a clown and needs to have a job at the office. He still feels like a clown so he still dresses as a clown. he keeps daydreaming about the moment he lost his job. but he just doesnt fit here.

About Project

Game Engine: Unreal engine 5

Code language: Blueprints

Platforms: Quest 2/3

Time: 8 weeks

People: 3 dev/1 artist

Description

We had to make this VR experience as our Artist was also our director for the game. So we had to make it how she wanted it to look like. It was a fun first look at how games are made for a client. i also learned a lot about unreal engine 5 and i do think that it is a really funny engine to use. I made this together with 2 other developers and 1 artist/director who also was a film academy student.
